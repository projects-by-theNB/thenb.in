Integration

All Modules Share One Data Backbone

No re-entering data between departments. A Sales Order triggers production planning. A Purchase Receipt updates inventory. HR data feeds payroll automatically.

Sales → Manufacturing

Confirming a Sales Order automatically raises a Work Order in Manufacturing. Production schedules update without a single phone call or email.

Purchase → QC → Inventory

A Purchase Receipt triggers an incoming QC inspection. Only passed material moves to inventory — rejected stock is quarantined automatically.

HR → Payroll → Finance

Attendance data from biometric devices flows directly to payroll. Salary calculations, statutory deductions, and bank files are generated without manual input.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the questions we hear most before a demo.

  • Does Navlakha ERP include HR and payroll?

    Yes. HR & Payroll is a core module of the ERP suite. It covers biometric attendance for all employee types — permanent staff, casual labour, and contract workers — automated payroll processing, PF/ESIC/PT/TDS/CLRA compliance, and an employee self-service mobile app.

  • Can I use only some modules and add more later?

    Absolutely. Each module is independently deployable. Many clients start with HR & Purchase, then add Manufacturing, QC, and Asset Management as their operations scale. Since all modules share the same master data, there are no integration headaches when you add a new one.

  • Is this suitable for small and mid-size manufacturers?

    Yes — this is exactly who it is built for. Navlakha ERP gives Indian SMEs practical, usable features without the complexity and cost of enterprise systems like SAP or Oracle. It is deployed in weeks, not months, and priced within reach of businesses with 10 to 1,000 employees.

  • How long does implementation take?

    Most implementations are live within 2–8 weeks depending on the number of modules and data migration complexity. Our team handles the entire setup, configuration, training, and go-live — so your internal team does not need to manage a separate IT project.

  • Is the ERP cloud-based or can we deploy on-premise?

    Both options are available. We offer a fully managed cloud deployment (SaaS) for quick setup and low maintenance, as well as on-premise deployment for organisations with data residency or compliance requirements. We recommend cloud for most clients.
