FAQ

Daily Labour HRMS — Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

  • What is Daily Labour HRMS software?

    Daily Labour HRMS software automates attendance tracking, muster roll generation, daily wage calculation, and payslip delivery for daily-wage workers. It replaces paper attendance registers and cash queues with a biometric-driven, fully digital system — reducing payroll processing from days to minutes.

  • Does it support piece-rate and hourly wage workers?

    Yes. SalaryMitra handles piece-rate, hourly, and daily wage structures. Different worker categories within the same business can run on different pay structures — all calculated automatically from attendance and production data. Overtime, allowances, and advance deductions are handled automatically too.

  • How are muster rolls generated?

    Muster rolls are generated automatically from biometric or QR attendance data. Every day's register shows each worker's attendance, hours worked, and wages payable. The register can be exported in the format required for statutory inspections — no manual filling, no missing entries.

  • Do workers need a smartphone or app?

    No. Workers only need a basic phone with WhatsApp to receive their payslips. Attendance is handled via biometric machines or QR codes at the worksite — no smartphone app required. Workers are kept informed about their wages without needing any digital literacy.

  • What's the pricing for Daily Labour HRMS?

    Daily labour management starts with our Professional plan at ₹30 per employee per month. For large daily-wage workforces, we offer volume pricing. WhatsApp us for a quote based on your headcount.
