Works with Any Biometric Device

Compatible with every hardware biometric brand — ZKTeco, eSSL, Realand, Suprema, and more. Fingerprint, face recognition, or card-based. No need to buy new devices.

PF, ESIC, Professional Tax, TDS, and CLRA — computed and filed automatically every payroll run. Zero manual errors, zero penalties. India's most complete statutory compliance built in.
