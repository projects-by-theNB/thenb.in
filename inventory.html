FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Inventory Management system.

  • Can it handle multiple warehouses and branches?

    Yes. The platform supports unlimited warehouses and branches. Each warehouse has its own stock ledger, and you can do inter-warehouse transfers with full audit trail. Management gets a consolidated real-time view across all locations.

  • Does it support barcode and QR code scanning?

    Yes. The web app and mobile app both support barcode and QR code scanning for receiving, issuing, and stock-take operations. You can use any standard USB barcode scanner on the desktop or the phone camera on mobile.

  • Can suppliers submit quotations online through a portal?

    Yes. We provide a supplier self-service portal where vendors can log in, view RFQs, and submit their quotations. This eliminates email back-and-forth and gives you automatic price comparison across suppliers.

  • Does it track expiry dates and send alerts?

    Yes. Each batch can have an expiry date. The system sends configurable advance alerts (e.g., 30/60/90 days before expiry) so you can take action before stock becomes unusable. Essential for food & beverage, agriculture, and any expiry-sensitive business.

  • Can it integrate with Tally or other accounting software?

    Yes. We offer integration with Tally, SAP, and other accounting systems. Purchase and stock data can be exported in formats compatible with your accounting software, ensuring your financial books always reflect actual inventory.
