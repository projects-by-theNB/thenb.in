How It Works

Set Up Once, Run on Autopilot

Depreciation and maintenance reminders run automatically — you just act on the alerts.

1

Register Assets

Add every asset with its purchase date, cost, category, location, and QR/barcode tag. Attach invoices, photos, and warranty documents directly.

2

Configure Depreciation

Set depreciation method (SLM or WDV), useful life, and residual value per asset category. Monthly depreciation entries run automatically.

3

Schedule Maintenance

Define preventive maintenance intervals — daily, monthly, annually, or by operating hours. Get reminders before the due date, not after a breakdown.

4

Track & Report

View asset health, maintenance cost history, AMC expiry, and downtime trends. Make data-driven decisions on repair vs. replace — before costs spiral.

  • Centralised asset register with categories
  • QR code & barcode tagging per asset

  • Document attachment (invoices, warranty, photos)

Depreciation

  • SLM (Straight Line Method) & WDV (Written Down Value)
  • Auto monthly depreciation entries

Maintenance & AMC

  • Warranty expiry alerts

Lifecycle & Analytics

  • Asset allocation, transfer & return tracking
  • Disposal, write-off & sale recording
  • Total maintenance cost per asset
  • Downtime analysis & MTBF reporting
  • Net book value & asset ageing reports
Why It Matters

No More Surprise Breakdowns.

What businesses gain when asset management is automated.

Maintain Before It Breaks

Preventive maintenance reminders ensure your machines are serviced on schedule — before a breakdown halts production and causes costly emergency repairs.

Know Where Every Asset Is

QR-tagged assets are always traceable. Scan to check current location, custodian, service history, and remaining warranty — instantly, from your phone.

Accurate Financial Records

Automated SLM/WDV depreciation means your books always reflect the true net book value of your assets — audit-ready, always consistent, no manual calculations.
