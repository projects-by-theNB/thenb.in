Inspection Gates

Quality at Every Stage

Three mandatory checkpoints ensure nothing substandard moves forward.

Incoming Inspection

Inspect raw materials and components at the time of receipt. Pass, reject, or hold — before they enter your warehouse.

In-Process Inspection

Conduct quality checks at defined stages of production. Catch defects mid-process so rework cost stays low.

Outgoing Inspection

Final QC before dispatch. Generate a Certificate of Analysis (CoA) and release only goods that meet specification.

How It Works

From Defect to Resolution

Every non-conformance is captured, investigated, and closed — no loose ends.

1

Run Inspection

Execute inspection against configurable checklists. Record measurements, observations, and pass/fail per parameter.

2

Raise NCR

If a defect is found, raise a Non-Conformance Report. Classify by defect type, root cause, and responsible party — and assign corrective action.

3

Analyse & Act

Review batch-wise rejection trends and supplier quality scores. Use the data to drive supplier development and process improvements.

4

Release with CoA

Once outgoing inspection passes, generate a Certificate of Analysis automatically and attach it to the delivery — ready to share with your customer.

Features

Everything in the Quality Control Module

Structured QC that prevents defects from reaching the next stage — or your customer.

Inspection Checklists

  • Configurable checklists per item & process stage
  • Numeric, visual, and pass/fail parameters
  • Upper & lower tolerance limits
  • Photo capture during inspection
  • Checklist version control

NCR & Root Cause

  • Non-Conformance Report (NCR) raising
  • Defect classification & severity tagging
  • Root cause analysis (5-Why framework)
  • Corrective & preventive action (CAPA) tracking
  • NCR closure workflow with approvals

Rejection Analysis

  • Batch-wise rejection rates & trends
  • Defect Pareto charts
  • Supplier-wise quality scorecards
  • Cost of poor quality (COPQ) tracking
  • Month-on-month quality trend reports

CoA & Compliance

  • Certificate of Analysis (CoA) auto-generation
  • Batch-linked CoA attached to delivery
  • Customisable CoA templates per product
  • Digital signature & stamp support
  • CoA archive & customer-wise history
Why It Matters
