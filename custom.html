FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our custom development engagements.

  • How long does a custom project typically take?

    Timelines range from 4 weeks for a focused automation tool to 6 months for a comprehensive enterprise system. After the discovery phase, we provide a detailed project plan with milestones, delivery dates, and payment schedules — so there are no surprises.

  • Do you provide ongoing maintenance after the project is delivered?

    Yes. We offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) that include bug fixes, minor enhancements, security updates, and priority support. Most clients continue with us on an ongoing basis as their digital transformation partner.

  • Can you integrate with our existing software like Tally or SAP?

    Yes. We have experience integrating with Tally, SAP, Busy, Zoho, and other popular business applications. We build secure APIs and middleware layers that allow your new custom software to communicate seamlessly with existing systems.

  • Do you sign an NDA to protect our project idea?

    Absolutely. We sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before any detailed discussion of your project requirements or business processes. Your intellectual property is yours — we build it, you own it.

  • What technologies and programming languages do you use?

    Our stack includes React, Node.js, Python, Flutter (for mobile), PostgreSQL, MySQL, and cloud platforms like AWS and Google Cloud. We choose the best technology for your specific requirements — not what is most familiar to us. All source code is delivered to you.
