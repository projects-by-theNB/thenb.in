FAQ

Contractor Management — Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

  • What is a Contractor Management HRMS?

    A Contractor Management HRMS is software that helps principal employers track attendance, wages, and compliance for contract labour supplied by third-party contractors. It replaces paper muster rolls and manual wage sheets with a fully automated, audit-ready system covering PF, ESIC, CLRA registers, gate passes, and vendor payments.

  • Does SalaryMitra handle CLRA compliance automatically?

    Yes. SalaryMitra automates all CLRA (Contract Labour Regulation & Abolition Act) requirements — Form 13 muster rolls, wages register, deductions register, and half-yearly returns. Compliance alerts notify you 30 days before every deadline so you never face inspection penalties. We also keep the system updated with state-specific minimum wage revisions.

  • Can I manage multiple contractors across different sites?

    Yes. SalaryMitra supports unlimited contractors across multiple sites. Each contractor has their own wage structure, compliance settings, and payment sheet. Senior management sees a consolidated dashboard across all locations, while site managers see only their own site data — with role-based access control.

  • How does the gate pass and site access system work?

    Contract workers are enrolled with Aadhaar verification and biometric data. SalaryMitra generates digital gate passes that are validated at the entry point. Only workers with valid, unexpired passes can enter. All entry/exit records flow automatically into the attendance and wage system — eliminating ghost workers and inflated headcounts.

  • What's the pricing for Contractor Management HRMS?

    Contractor management is included in our Enterprise plan. Pricing starts at ₹45 per employee per month. For large contractor workforces, we offer volume pricing. WhatsApp us for a custom quote based on your headcount and number of sites.
