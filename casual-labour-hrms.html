The Problem

Why Casual Workforce Management Gets Chaotic

Casual and seasonal workers need different systems — and most HRMS tools weren't built for them.

✗

No Worker Pool

Every peak season, managers scramble to find available workers by making phone calls — with no record of who's skilled, reliable, or available.

✗

Irregular Shift Chaos

Tracking on-call workers across irregular shifts with different rates and hours using Excel always ends in errors, disputes, and wasted time.

✗

Re-enrolment Every Season

Without a system, seasonal workers must be re-registered every season — wasting time and leading to errors in bank account and Aadhaar details.

✗

Compliance Uncertainty

No clear legislation for casual workers means businesses don't know what's required — and risk penalties for minimum wage or PF non-compliance.

FAQ

Casual Labour Management — Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

  • What is Casual Labour Management software?

    Casual Labour Management software helps businesses manage on-call, seasonal, and irregularly-scheduled workers. It maintains a pool of available workers by skill, automates flexible shift scheduling, tracks irregular hours accurately, and handles statutory compliance for the casual labour category — replacing spreadsheets and manual call-up processes.

  • How does seasonal workforce scaling work in SalaryMitra?

    SalaryMitra maintains a persistent worker pool. During peak season, mark workers as active, assign them shifts, and start tracking attendance — all from the dashboard. Off-season, mark them inactive. Their biometrics, bank details, and history are retained, so next season's activation takes seconds rather than hours of re-registration.

  • Can it calculate pay accurately for irregular and partial shifts?

    Yes. SalaryMitra handles any pay structure — half-day, partial shift, on-call allowance, night differential, and overtime for casual workers. Each shift's biometric attendance data flows automatically into wage calculation. Workers are paid exactly for the hours and shifts they worked, with a complete audit trail for every payment.

  • What statutory compliance does SalaryMitra cover for casual workers?

    SalaryMitra covers minimum wage compliance for the casual category (with state-wise auto-updates), PF and ESIC applicability based on actual days worked, attendance and wage registers for labour inspections, and compliance threshold alerts when a casual worker's engagement pattern triggers statutory registration requirements.

  • What's the pricing for Casual Labour Management HRMS?

    Casual labour management is included in the Enterprise plan starting at ₹45 per employee per month. For businesses with large seasonal swings, we offer flexible pricing based on peak headcount. WhatsApp us for a custom quote.
