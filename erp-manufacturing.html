How It Works

Shop Floor to Finished Goods

Every production run is planned, tracked, and costed with complete visibility.

1

Define Bill of Materials

Build multi-level BOMs for every product — raw materials, sub-assemblies, and packaging. One BOM drives all downstream planning automatically.

2

Create Work Orders

Raise work orders against sales orders or a production plan. Assign machines, workstations, and operators — and schedule across shifts.

3

Track Shop Floor

Record material consumption, machine runtime, and yield at each stage. Spot bottlenecks in real time before they impact delivery dates.

4

Receive & Analyse

Record finished goods receipts and scrap. Get production cost per unit, efficiency rates, and variance reports — so you know exactly where every rupee went.
