FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

  • Does it support casual / contract labour?

    Yes. The system has a dedicated casual labour module that handles daily-wage workers, muster rolls, and CLRA (Contract Labour Regulation & Abolition) compliance. You can manage daily attendance, weekly wages, and generate all required statutory registers.

  • Which biometric devices are supported?

    We natively support all major biometric brands including ZKTeco, eSSL, Suprema, Matrix, and more. Our team handles the integration setup, and you can manage all devices remotely from a central dashboard.

  • Can it handle multiple branches and entities?

    Absolutely. The platform is built for multi-branch, multi-entity organisations. Each entity can have its own payroll structure, compliance settings, and reporting while senior management gets a consolidated view across all locations.

  • Is WhatsApp salary slip delivery available?

    Yes! Salary slips can be automatically generated and sent to each employee's WhatsApp number at the click of a button — no manual emailing required. Employees who don't use email receive their slips instantly on WhatsApp.

  • Does it handle weekly or biweekly payroll cycles?

    Yes. The system supports daily, weekly, biweekly, and monthly payroll cycles. Different employee groups within the same company can run on different payroll frequencies — for example, daily workers on weekly payroll and staff on monthly payroll.

  • Is it cloud-based or can we deploy on-premise?

    Both options are available. We offer a fully managed cloud deployment (SaaS) for quick setup and low maintenance, as well as on-premise deployment for organisations with data residency or compliance requirements. Our team supports both models.
