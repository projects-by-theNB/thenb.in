FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Sales Tracking & CRM.

  • Does the sales rep know they are being tracked?

    Yes. Tracking is fully transparent. Sales reps know they are being tracked once they log in to the app. Tracking only activates during active work sessions. We believe transparent tracking builds trust and improves accountability rather than creating surveillance anxiety.

  • Does it record all phone calls automatically?

    Yes, with proper device permissions enabled. The app records customer calls made and received on the registered work phone. Recordings are stored securely and accessible only to authorised managers. The feature complies with applicable regulations when disclosures are made.

  • Can I see call transcriptions?

    Yes. AI transcription is available as an add-on. Calls are automatically transcribed and key highlights such as commitments, follow-ups, and objections are extracted — saving managers hours of call review time.

  • Does it work without internet connectivity?

    Yes. The mobile app has an offline mode that stores activity data locally. When internet connectivity is restored, all data is automatically synced to the server. This ensures sales reps in low-connectivity areas don't lose any activity logs.

  • Can it integrate with WhatsApp for customer communication?

    Yes. WhatsApp integration is available for sending automated follow-up messages, sharing brochures, and updating customers. Sales reps can initiate WhatsApp chats directly from the customer card in the CRM, and conversations are logged automatically.
