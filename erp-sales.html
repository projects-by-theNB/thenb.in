Sales Flow

One-click conversion at every stage. No re-entering data. Full audit trail from enquiry to payment.

How It Works

From Enquiry to Payment

Every step tracked and connected — no loose ends, no missed follow-ups.

1

Build a Quotation

Create professional quotations with item-wise pricing, taxes, discounts and validity period. Send directly to the customer via email or WhatsApp.

2

Confirm Sales Order

When the customer accepts, convert the quotation to a Sales Order in one click. Stock is reserved, production or dispatch is notified automatically.

3

Raise Invoice

Generate GST-compliant invoices directly from the Sales Order. Partial billing, advance adjustments, and credit notes — all handled seamlessly.

4

Track Receivables

Monitor outstanding payments, send payment reminders, and view ageing reports by customer — so you always know who owes what and since when.
